Simran’s Favorite Musical Artists

About This Page

I am a huge music lover. I think music is one of those magical things that can alter your mood almost immediately. Over the years, I have fallen in love with 2 specific musical artists and am obsessed with so many of their songs. Now, I might be biased, but I think these are some of the best, most versatile artists in the industry, and their music is definitely worth listening to. I want to share these artists’ magic with all of you and hope to persuade you to give them a listen!

Who Are My Favorites?

COLDPLAY

Coldplay is a British alternative rock (now closer to pop rock) group whose career has spanned almost two decades. The group’s members are: Chris Martin - lead singer and pianist, Jonny Buckland - guitarist, Guy Berryman - bassist, and Will Champion - drummer. The band has released 7 hit albums and is known for their vibrant live performances and concert tours. They recently headlined the Superbowl 50 halftime show in 2015. They also just released a film last week which documents the band’s 20 years together.

Last year, I had the honor of attending their concert in Boston. “Seeing Coldplay live” has been on my bucket list for years, and it was everything I dreamed of and more. The music, the staging and props, and the atmosphere were all phenomenal. What a transcendent experience. I think everyone should see Coldplay live at least once in their lifetime!

Coldplay - Awards and Achievements

  • 7 Grammy Awards (34 nominations)
  • 7 Billboard Music Awards (24 nominations)
  • 9 Brit Awards (27 nominations)
  • 6 MTV Video Music Awards (20 nominations)

CÉLINE DION

Céline Dion is widely regarded as one of the best vocalists of our generation. Born in Quebec, she sings both in English and French. Dion is known for her ballads and originally became famous for her iconic performance on the Titanic soundtrack. Her career has now spanned almost 3 decades, but she doesn’t seem to be stopping anytime soon. Since 2015, she has held a residency at The Colosseum at Caesars Palace in Las Vegas.

While I have never seen Céline Dion perform live, it is at the top of my bucket list (now that I’ve already checked Coldplay off). Her voice speaks volumes to me, and her personality seems so charming. I have spent countless hours watching her live performances and documentaries online. She is definitely one of the greats.

Céline Dion - Awards and Achievements

  • 5 Grammy Awards (17 nominations)
  • 7 Billboard Music Awards (41 nominations)
  • 7 American Music Awards (15 nominations)
  • 20 JUNO - Canadian Music Awards (76 nominations)


Watch my favorite Céline Dion live performance here:
Céline Dion - My Heart Will Go On (Live on Billboard Music Awards 2017)
